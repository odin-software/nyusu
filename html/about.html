About Nyusu

Nyusu is a simple and clean RSS feed reader built with Go. It provides a web interface to manage your RSS feeds, bookmark posts, and stay up to date with your favorite content.

Features

  • Subscribe to RSS feeds
  • Bookmark your favorite posts
  • Clean, mobile-friendly interface
  • Automatic feed updates

Source

Nyusu is developed and hosted by Odin Software.
